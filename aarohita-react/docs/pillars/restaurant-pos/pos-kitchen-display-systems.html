Kitchen Display Systems & POS

Kitchen Display Systems (KDS) replace paper tickets with digital screens that show orders in real time. When tightly integrated with your POS, a KDS improves order routing, reduces mistakes and shortens ticket times — which directly improves guest satisfaction and throughput.

Why KDS matters for restaurants

Modern kitchens run on timing and coordination. A KDS provides:

  • Clear visual routing of orders by station and priority
  • Automated acknowledgements to prevent missed tickets
  • Order timers and visual alerts for delays
  • Better data for kitchen performance and training

Integrating KDS with POS

Integrations typically push orders from the POS to the KDS in structured formats (itemized lines, modifiers, prep notes). Choose a POS that supports realtime webhooks or a native KDS integration. Test edge cases: voids, refunds and reprints to ensure the KDS stays in sync.
