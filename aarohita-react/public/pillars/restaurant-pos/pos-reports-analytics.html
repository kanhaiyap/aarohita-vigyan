POS Reporting & Analytics

Actionable reporting turns daily transactions into strategic decisions. A modern POS should provide sales by item, hourly throughput, table turn, void and refund analysis, and labor cost dashboards to help managers optimise staffing and menus.

High-value reports to track

  • Sales by item & category: remove low-performing dishes and promote high-margin items.
  • Sales by hour / shift: staff appropriately for peak periods.
  • Voids and refunds: monitor patterns to reduce loss and identify training needs.
  • Inventory vs sales variance: detect shrinkage and improve ordering accuracy.

Data quality & Bhojan Mitra

Bhojan Mitra improves data quality by capturing accurate, multilingual orders and reducing manual mistakes. Higher-quality POS input enables better forecasting and reliable dashboards.

Visualisations & alerting
