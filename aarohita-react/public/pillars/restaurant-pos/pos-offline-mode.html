Offline Mode & Reliability for POS

Network outages happen. A robust POS should continue accepting orders, print receipts and sync once connectivity returns. Aarohita Vigyan's POS guidance explains offline-first design patterns and operational checks that keep restaurants running.

Why offline mode matters

For restaurants, even short downtimes mean lost revenue. Offline mode ensures orders are captured locally and reconciled later — critical for high-volume kitchens and cloud kitchens alike.

Bhojan Mitra & offline resilience

When integrated with Bhojan Mitra — our voice-enabled assistant — offline flows should cache voice commands locally and confirm orders on reconnection, preventing duplicate charges and ensuring customer trust.

Quick checklist

  • Local data queueing with retry and deduplication
  • Graceful fallback UI for staff
  • Safe storage & encryption of queued orders
