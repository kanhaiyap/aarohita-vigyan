Essential POS System Features for Restaurants

Modern restaurant POS platforms are modular: the core order-taking and payments modules must be rock-solid, while add-ons such as loyalty, CRM and analytics provide operational leverage. Focus first on reliability and then on automations that reduce manual work.

Core modules (must-have)

  • Order entry & menu management: fast SKU lookup, modifiers, portion control and templates for popular combos.
  • Payments & settlement: UPI, cards, wallets and automatic settlement reporting; support for partial payments and split bills.
  • Offline resilience: local queuing, safe storage and idempotent reconciliation to avoid duplicate orders.
  • Inventory & recipe mapping: ingredient-level tracking, waste capture and automatic re-order triggers.
  • Kitchen routing & KDS: ticket routing, prep timers and prioritisation for multi-station kitchens.

Operational add-ons (high ROI)

  • Loyalty & CRM: customer profiles, rewards and targeted offers based on POS events.
  • Delivery & marketplace integrations: auto-ingest orders from aggregators and avoid manual entry errors.
  • Reporting & analytics: scheduled reports, anomaly detection and drill-down dashboards.
  • APIs & webhooks: enable headless integrations and custom automations (for example, with Bhojan Mitra).

Checklist for vendor evaluation

Ask vendors for:

  • Sample API docs and webhook examples
  • Reference sites in your city/segment
